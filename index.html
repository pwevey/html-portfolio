Paul Wever

Technical Support Manager at BobCAD-CAM


I am the Manager of the Technical Services department at BobCAD-CAM. BobCAD-CAM is a CAD-CAM software that was created in 1985. The software supports Milling machines (2.5 up to 5 axis), Lathes, Mill-Turn, Wire EDM, Laser, Plasma and Waterjet. CAD-CAM software is used in the manufacturing industry to create programs that post out G and M codes to get sent and run on those machines. This process is called, "CNC" or Computer Numeric Control and is used for precision machining where the tolerance of the parts that get produced can be anywhere from +/- 0.005" (in industry terms: plus or minus 5 "thou") for Milling machines and +/- 0.0001" (One tenth of a "thou") for Wire EDM machines. To put into perspective, the thickness of a human hair is around 0.002" or 2 "thou".
